Short Bio

I am a Researcher at the Data Science Department of NEC Labs. I received my Ph.D. degree from College of IST, The Pennsylvania State University in 2018, advised by Prof. Xiang Zhang. My research interests are data mining and machine learning, with a particular focus on the analysis of graph-structured data and sequential data, as well as their application in different areas.
